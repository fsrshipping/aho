Our mission

Global rights for LGBTI's

LGBTI rights are not a fact for everybody in this colorful world. We want that to change

A'HO

We call ourselves African Homosexual organisation, a.k.a. A'HO and we are proud for being unique and expressing ourselves. We are a small group of active A'HOs in the USA combining forces to make this world a better place where everybody is accepted for who they are.

Gays in Africa

In many Western countries being A'HO is accepted and coming out of the closet is encouraged. In many countries A'HO men and woman are suppressed and prosecuted. Our organisation targets especially A'HO man and woman in African countries.

We help A'HOs

How can we help? First of all we are here for moral support but we know that it is not enough. We know that it's all about action. You want to be free to express yourself and show the world who you are. That's why we support projects in Africa that are actively promoting A'HO rights in their country.

A'HO Activists

There are already active A'HOs in Africa. Each promoting our cause and fighting for rights for A'HOs in their own and unique way.
